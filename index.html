Field Service Operations

From guesswork to precision. 

Technician dispatch has always been a game of chance—send the wrong person, and you’re looking at delays, repeat visits, and frustrated customers. 

Predictive AI changes that. By analyzing past fixes, skills, and even equipment history, it ensures the right technician arrives with the right tools the first time. The result? A 60% boost in first-time fix rates. For industries like manufacturing, medical equipment, and labs, that means less downtime, safer operations, and happier clients.

Partner Management

Complex ecosystems, simplified.

OEMs live in a world of sprawling partner networks—dealers, distributors, repair shops—all working at different speeds. Managing them often eats up more time than the work itself. 

AI-powered coordination cuts through the noise, automating scheduling, routing, and communication. The payoff? A 50% reduction in partner management overhead. For white goods, electronics, and automotive industries, it turns a messy web into a smooth, synchronized machine.

Site Management

Taming complexity across environments. 

A manufacturing plant or hospital isn’t one system—it’s dozens of interconnected ones. When something breaks, finding the root cause can take hours or days. 

AI thrives in this complexity. By correlating issues across products and environments, it accelerates resolution by 40%. For data centers, plants, and hospitals, that means fewer blind spots, faster fixes, and smoother operations where every minute matters.
